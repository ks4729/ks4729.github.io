Paradigm Robotics

Engineering intern supporting robotics hardware development and thermal management systems

Overview

Worked on hardware development for robotics systems with focus on manufacturing, thermal management, and materials characterization. Contributed to component fabrication, thermal analysis, and electronics integration.

Key Responsibilities

Manufacturing & Machining

Fabricated precision components using CNC mills and lathes. Maintained tight tolerances for mechanical assemblies and fixtures. Developed fixturing solutions for complex part geometries.

Thermal Management

Conducted thermal analysis and testing of phase change materials (PCMs) for electronics cooling applications. Evaluated heat dissipation strategies and implemented thermal shielding solutions to protect sensitive electronics from heat-generating components.

Materials Analysis

Characterized thermal properties of phase change materials under various operating conditions. Tested material performance for thermal buffering and heat sink applications in robotics hardware.

Electronics Integration

Performed soldering and assembly of electronic components. Implemented thermal protection solutions for circuit boards and power electronics. Assisted with cable routing and connector assembly for robot systems.

Skills Developed

  • Thermal analysis and heat transfer fundamentals
  • Materials characterization and testing protocols

  • CAD/CAM for manufacturing planning
